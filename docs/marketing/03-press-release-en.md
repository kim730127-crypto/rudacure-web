**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**INCHEON, South Korea — [DATE]** — RudaCure Co., Ltd., a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company developing first-in-class non-opioid therapeutics for pain and sensory disorders, today announced significant progress on its lead asset RCI001, including U.S. FDA Phase 2 IND clearance, a strategic memorandum of understanding with WuXi AppTec, and preparation for an IPO on Korea's KOSDAQ exchange.

**RCI001 — A Differentiated Mechanism for Dry Eye Disease**

RCI001 (currently in U.S. FDA Phase 2; ClinicalTrials.gov Identifier: NCT07068958) is a TRPV1-Rac1 downstream signal modulator developed for moderate-to-severe dry eye disease. Unlike direct TRPV1 channel antagonists, which can cause off-target thermosensation effects, RCI001 selectively modulates the TRPV1 downstream Rac1/NLRP3 inflammatory cascade — restoring tear film homeostasis and corneal epithelial healing without blocking heat perception.

In preclinical studies on Sjögren's syndrome models, a single 0.25% topical ophthalmic solution restored tear secretion within one week and produced therapeutic effects within four weeks — significantly faster than traditional steroid-based treatments and without the side effects of intraocular pressure elevation, redness, or burning.

"The dry eye field has seen multiple TRPV1-targeted approvals in 2025, but each addresses only the symptomatic channel," said Dr. Yong-ho Kim, CEO and Founder of RudaCure. "RCI001's downstream modulation strategy targets the inflammatory root cause. We believe this is what makes RCI001 a true next-generation candidate, not another iteration."

**Strategic WuXi AppTec Partnership**

RudaCure recently signed a strategic memorandum of understanding with WuXi AppTec, the world's largest contract development and manufacturing organization, at WuXi's Jinshan facility in China. The partnership connects RudaCure to WuXi's global clinical development network, accelerating the path from preclinical to multinational trials.

**Pipeline Beyond RCI001**

- **RCI002** — A TRPV1-MOR biased dual-target non-opioid analgesic for chronic pain. Currently in IND-enabling studies with FDA Orphan Drug Designation in preparation for Complex Regional Pain Syndrome (CRPS). RudaCure presented preclinical data at the 2025 Society for Neuroscience Annual Meeting.
- **RCI003** — A psoriasis candidate in discovery, jointly developed with Syntekabio's STB AI platform.
- **RCI0165** — An osteoarthritis program leveraging RudaCure's RuCIA AI drug discovery technology.

**RuCIA AI Drug Discovery Platform**

RudaCure's proprietary RuCIA platform applies machine learning to ion channel structure-activity prediction, reducing candidate identification time and cost by up to 70% compared to conventional approaches. The platform powers all four RudaCure pipelines and is being expanded to additional sensory disease targets.

**Specialized Preclinical CRO Services**

In parallel with internal pipeline development, RudaCure operates a dedicated preclinical CRO offering ion channel-specialized services to other biopharmaceutical companies. The CRO has completed 19+ contract studies, operates 13+ disease animal models, and provides FLIPR Penta high-throughput screening for TRPV1, TRPA1, Nav1.7/1.8, and hERG cardiac safety.

**Funding and Path to IPO**

RudaCure has raised over KRW 16 billion in equity through Series A and Series B rounds, and signed over KRW 70 billion in licensing deals — including a Hanlim Pharmaceutical agreement for RCI001/RCI001U domestic rights and a French veterinary pharmaceutical partner for RCI001AH animal health applications. The company selected its IPO underwriter in late 2025 and is targeting a KOSDAQ listing.

**About RudaCure Co., Ltd.**

Founded in 2018 by Dr. Yong-ho Kim of Gachon University College of Medicine, RudaCure is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company specializing in ion channel-targeted therapeutics for pain and sensory disorders. The company is headquartered in Incheon, South Korea, with a research center in Seoul. RudaCure was selected as a 2024 Baby Unicorn by the Korean Ministry of SMEs and Startups and as an Incheon Outstanding Company in 2025. Learn more at https://www.rudacure.com.
